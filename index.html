Permissions & Privacy

MJ PDF Reader is designed by Mudlej with a privacy-first approach. We believe your documents are yours alone.

🔒 Zero Data Collection

This app does not track your activity, does not have analytics, and does not upload your files to any server.


Required Permissions:

  • Internet: Only used for opening PDFs via URL links.
  • Storage: Required to save downloaded PDFs and open files from your device storage.
