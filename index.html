About the App

What is MJ PDF Reader?

MJ PDF Reader is a fast, minimalist, powerful and completely free PDF viewer for Android, created by the developer Mudlej. It is proudly open-source and available on GitLab for anyone to inspect, use, or contribute to.

Unlike bloated PDF readers that demand excessive permissions, show ads, or quietly harvest your data, MJ PDF Reader was built with a single focus: reading PDFs quickly and privately. At just 15 MB, it's one of the smallest full-featured PDF viewers available for Android.

With features like blazing-fast search, dark mode, Text Mode, auto scroll, table of contents, and hyperlink support — MJ PDF punches far above its weight.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MJ PDF Reader — features, privacy, installation, and more.

What is MJ PDF Reader?

+
MJ PDF Reader is a fast, minimalist, powerful, and completely free PDF viewer for Android developed by Mudlej. It is 100% open source and available on GitLab. It features dark mode, blazing-fast search, Text Mode for plain-text reading, auto scroll, table of contents, hyperlink support, multi-instance support, and the ability to open online PDFs — all packed into just 15 MB with zero data collection.

Does MJ PDF Reader collect any personal data?

+
No. MJ PDF Reader does not collect any data whatsoever. The app contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising frameworks, and makes no network requests to third-party servers. The only network activity is when you explicitly open a PDF via a URL. Everything you read, every document you open, and your reading preferences all stay entirely on your device.

What permissions does MJ PDF Reader require?

+
MJ PDF Reader requests only two permissions: (1) Internet — required for opening PDFs through URLs and links. Not used for advertising or tracking. (2) Storage — required for saving downloaded PDFs and for opening PDFs from your device's local storage. No other permissions are requested or required.

Is MJ PDF Reader free and open source?

+
Yes, MJ PDF Reader is completely free to download and use, with no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no premium tiers. The entire source code is publicly available on GitLab, allowing anyone to inspect the code, verify the privacy claims, report issues, or contribute improvements.

What Android version is needed to run MJ PDF Reader?

+
MJ PDF Reader requires Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher. It targets Android 14 and supports four processor architectures: arm64-v8a (most modern phones), armeabi-v7a (older ARM devices), x86, and x86_64 (Intel-based tablets and emulators). This broad compatibility means it works on the vast majority of Android devices currently in use.

Can I open PDFs from the internet or online links?

+
Yes. MJ PDF Reader supports opening PDFs directly through URLs and online links. You can paste a PDF URL and the app will open it directly. You can also save/download the online PDF to your device storage for offline access later. This is what the Internet permission is used for.

Does MJ PDF Reader need root access?

+
No, root access is not required. MJ PDF Reader installs and runs on standard, non-rooted Android devices. You simply need to enable "Install from Unknown Sources" temporarily in your device security settings during APK installation, and then re-disable it afterward for best security practice.

What is Text Mode in MJ PDF Reader?

+
Text Mode extracts and displays the text content of a PDF as plain readable text, stripping away all PDF formatting and graphics. This is ideal for reading text-heavy documents, e-books, or articles in a clean, distraction-free view. In Text Mode you can customize the font size, text color, and background color. All your preferences and your last page position are saved automatically for each document.

How do I enable dark mode?

+
Dark mode can be toggled directly from within the app's reading view. Tap the menu icon while a PDF is open, and select the Dark Mode option to switch between light and dark themes. The app will remember your preference across sessions automatically.

Can I open multiple PDFs at the same time?

+
Yes. MJ PDF Reader supports multi-instance mode, allowing you to open multiple PDF documents simultaneously. The app also remembers the last read page for each individual document, so you can switch between multiple files and always return to exactly where you left off.

How does auto scroll work?

+
Auto Scroll enables hands-free reading by automatically scrolling through the PDF at a speed you set. You can scroll forward or backward. The speed is fully adjustable to match your reading pace. Auto scroll automatically stops and exits when you leave Full Screen Mode, ensuring you stay in control at all times.

Is it safe to install MJ PDF Reader as an APK?

+
Yes. MJ PDF Reader is completely open source — you can inspect every line of code on GitLab before installing. The APK is the exact same build distributed on alternative app stores. We recommend downloading only from the official link on this page or from the GitLab releases page to ensure authenticity. You can also scan the APK with an antivirus app before installation for extra assurance.

Does MJ PDF Reader support password-protected PDFs?

+
MJ PDF Reader uses Android's built-in PDF rendering engine, which supports standard PDF features. For password-protected PDFs, the app will prompt you to enter the password to unlock and view the document. Functionality may vary depending on the encryption type used in the PDF.

Can I share or print PDFs from MJ PDF Reader?

+
Yes. MJ PDF Reader allows you to share PDFs directly from the app using Android's standard share sheet, enabling you to send documents via email, messaging apps, or cloud storage. Printing is also supported through Android's built-in print system — just select Print from the share or options menu.

How do I update MJ PDF Reader to the latest version?

+
Since MJ PDF Reader is installed as an APK outside the Play Store, it will not update automatically. To update, simply download the latest APK file from this page and install it over your existing installation — all your data, reading positions, and preferences will be preserved. You can check the version number in the app's About section.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2024. We take your privacy very seriously — here is exactly what we collect (and don't collect).

🛡️ Short version: MJ PDF Reader collects absolutely no personal data. Nothing. Zero. This policy explains what permissions we use and why.

1. Information We Collect

MJ PDF Reader does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information of any kind. There are no analytics SDKs, no advertising frameworks, no crash reporting libraries, and no third-party services integrated into the application.

We do not collect: your name, email address, device identifiers, IP address, location data, usage statistics, reading habits, file names, or any other personal or behavioral data.

2. Permissions Used

The app requests only two Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — Used solely to fetch PDFs when you provide a URL or tap an online PDF link. This permission is never used to contact advertising networks, analytics servers, or any third-party infrastructure.
  • READ/WRITE EXTERNAL STORAGE — Used solely to open PDF files stored on your device and to save downloaded PDFs. No files are accessed without explicit user action.
3. Data Storage

All data generated by the app (such as your last read page position, Text Mode preferences, and display settings) is stored locally on your device only using Android's standard SharedPreferences and file storage APIs. This data never leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

MJ PDF Reader does not integrate any third-party services, including but not limited to: Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook SDK, Crashlytics, AdMob, or any other advertising or analytics platform.

5. Children's Privacy

MJ PDF Reader does not collect any data from users of any age, including children under 13. The app is safe for use by all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

7. Contact
