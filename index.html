About the App

What is MJ PDF Reader?

MJ PDF Reader is a fast, minimalist, powerful and completely free PDF viewer for Android, created by the developer known as Mudlej. It is proudly open-source and available on GitLab for anyone to inspect, use, or contribute to.

Unlike bloated PDF readers that demand excessive permissions, show ads, or quietly harvest your data, MJ PDF Reader was built with a single focus: reading PDFs quickly and privately. It's a lightweight PDF reader that respects your device and your privacy.

With features like blazing-fast search, dark mode, Text Mode, auto scroll, table of contents, and hyperlink support — MJ PDF punches far above its weight.

Privacy & Permissions

Permissions & Privacy

MJ PDF Reader is built around privacy. It requests only the permissions needed to open and save PDFs.

🌐

Internet Access

Used only when you open PDFs via a URL. No trackers, no ads, no analytics usage.

📁

Storage Access

Needed to open local PDFs and save downloaded PDFs. Only files you select are accessed.

🛡️

Zero Data Collection

MJ PDF Reader does not collect personal data. No analytics SDKs, no advertising frameworks, and no third-party tracking. As an open-source PDF reader, you can verify this through the public code on GitLab.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about MJ PDF Reader APK, privacy, permissions, features, and installation.

What is MJ PDF Reader?

+
MJ PDF Reader is a fast, minimalist, powerful, and free PDF viewer for Android developed by Mudlej. It is open source and focuses on privacy, speed, and usability.

Does MJ PDF Reader collect any personal data?

+
No. MJ PDF Reader does not collect any data. There are no analytics SDKs, trackers, or advertising frameworks.

What permissions does MJ PDF Reader require?

+
Internet (only for opening PDFs by URL) and Storage (to open/save PDFs locally). No extra permissions are required.

Is MJ PDF Reader free and open source?

+
Yes. The app is free to use and the full source code is available on GitLab.

What Android version is needed?

+
MJ PDF Reader requires Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher and targets Android 14.

Can I open PDFs from the internet or online links?

+
Yes. It supports opening PDFs via direct URLs — this is why the Internet permission exists.

Does MJ PDF Reader need root access?

+
No. Root access is not required.

What is Text Mode in MJ PDF Reader?

+
Text Mode extracts and displays PDF text as plain reading content. You can customize font size and colors for comfortable reading.

Is MJ PDF Reader safe to install?

+
The app is open source and designed without trackers or ads. For best safety, download only from the official link in the Download section of this site.

Does it support very large PDF files?

+
Yes. MJ PDF Reader is optimized for performance and includes fast search and navigation for large documents.

Can I use MJ PDF Reader offline?

+
Yes. Local PDFs work fully offline. Internet is only needed when opening PDFs via URL.

Does it include ads or in-app purchases?

+
No. MJ PDF Reader is free with no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.

Will it remember my last page?

+
Yes. It automatically remembers where you left off for each PDF.

Where can I see the source code?

+
You can find the source code on GitLab: https://gitlab.com/mudlej_android/mj_pdf_reader

How do I uninstall the app?

+
Uninstall normally through Android Settings → Apps → MJ PDF Reader → Uninstall.
